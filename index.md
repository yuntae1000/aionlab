## AION Lab — AI-On-Chip Neuro-Intelligent Circuits

AION Lab is a research group at **Kyung Hee University (KHU)** focusing on
**AI-optimized analog/mixed-signal ICs** and **neuro-intelligent circuits** for real-world sensing and on-chip intelligence.

### Research Keywords
- Analog / Mixed-Signal IC
- Neural Interface ASIC (recording / stimulation)
- Low-noise AFE (CCIA/IA), ΔΣ ADC
- Compute-in-Memory (CIM/PIM)
- AIoT sensing SoCs
